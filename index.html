About Me

Hi all, I'm Ciara and I am the sole owner of Pawfection Parlour. A little bit about me: I have pretty much always worked with animals. From working in a horse riding stables, I then qualified as a veterinary nurse. During this time I also spent my summers working in a dog grooming salon. I then worked as a veterinary nurse for a few years before taking off travelling for 14 months. I spent most of my time travelling and working in Australia and also visited New Zealand, Sri Lanka, Vietnam, Cambodia, and Thailand. Upon arriving back in Ireland, I realised Dog Grooming was what I truly loved doing. I then started training with Rachel's Clip 'n' Tails where I continued learning all the valuable skills I know now. With the help of my Dad (Gerry) we renovated the lovely space that is now Pawfection Parlour. I officially opened in May 2020 and it has been a fantastic two years getting to know all your four-legged friends.

At Your Service

Grooming

My full groom gives your dog the best of everything, which includes; brush out, ear clean, nail clip, bubble bath, fluff dry and full body hair cut, styling, and spritz of doggie cologne.

Washing

Pamper your dog with a luxury wash and blow dry. A bubble bath includes fluff dry, brush out, ear clean, nail clip, shaved pads, eye trim, sanitary trim and a spritz of doggie cologne.
